## Agenda 1.16.25

Transition to Budget Withdrawal Operations: The committee confirmed the successful passing of the budget info action and is now focusing on the complex execution of 39 individual treasury withdrawals, including mainnet smart contract testing.

Committee Structure and Governance Reform: Discussions began on merging existing working groups, reforming the committee's terms of reference to clarify its scope, and exploring the implementation of spending horizon modeling.

External and Future Process Awareness: The committee addressed the need to monitor Utah's proposed constitutional changes and began planning for the 2026 budget process retrospective, including logistics for a hybrid event.
