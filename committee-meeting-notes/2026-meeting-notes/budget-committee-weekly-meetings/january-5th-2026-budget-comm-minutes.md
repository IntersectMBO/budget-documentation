### **3. Edits to Strengthen Constitutional Read and Rationale**

#### **3.1 Abstract Revision: NCL Applies to Treasury Withdrawals Only (Nico)**

* Nico flagged a constitutional interpretation issue:
  * The abstract sentence stating NCL applies to **both Budget Actions and Treasury Withdrawals** is likely incorrect.
  * From Nico’s constitutional perspective, **NCL governs Treasury withdrawals**, not budgets.
* Action agreed:
  * Remove/adjust that sentence to apply **only to Treasury withdrawals**.
* Nico offered to make the edit directly in the working Google Doc.

#### **3.2 Rationale Updates: “Unused 2025 NCL” No Longer Accurate (Nico)**

* Nico noted the rationale still references unused 2025 NCL amounts.
* With the **Critical Integrations budget** approved, the “unused” balance is now materially different (approx. **1–2M ADA** remaining, per Nico).
* Committee direction:
  * Update the rationale to remove outdated statements and reflect the current facts.

#### **3.3 Replace Forecasts with Actual Collections (Dimitri + Lloyd)**

* Nico requested Dimitri update the inflow figures:
  * It is now 2026, so the committee can state **actual 2025 inflows**, not forecasts.
* Dimitri agreed:
  * NCL should be grounded in **historic collections**, while forecasts can remain for education/long-term context.
* Lloyd reinforced the strategic intent:
  * The 18-month structure enables future NCLs to be **fully backward-looking** (e.g., 2027 NCL based on 2026 inflows).

#### **3.4 Supporting Attachments**

* Simo suggested including Dimitri’s calculations/forecasting material as supporting documentation (PDF available; IPFS not yet referenced in transcript).
* Lloyd indicated he can update the GovTool forum version directly as edits are finalized.
